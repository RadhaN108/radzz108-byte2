About This Data Set

Accountability

The information provided in the Building Data Reports is an indication of the free and reduced eligible students that have been reported by each Sponsor of the School Nutrition Programs (SNP), as well as their corresponding meal service sites/schools. The report provides both the free and reduced eligible approved students and the percentage of free and/or reduced per the enrollment.
